The S2S Index of Indexes™

An index demands a ranking. It follows, with grim inevitability, that the index of indexes must itself be ranked. We are nothing if not consistent.

Stones2Stars keeps several ranked catalogs. This page ranks the catalogs themselves, by their Load-Bearing Authority™ — a rigorously unscientific blend of how often each is the correct answer to "wait… that's intentional?", and how reliably it consumes a developer's afternoon. Click one to descend.

🥇 The Despair Index →

Authority 97 / 100

The flagship. Bugs ranked by the developer despair they induce, in centiphants. Opened mid-git blame, consulted in anguish, quoted at parties. The one everyone actually reads.

🥈 The Realism Index →

Authority 84 / 100

The balm. Absurdities working exactly as designed — "it's not a bug, it's gloriously realistic." Where despair goes to be reframed as a feature. Cathartic; faintly defensive.

🥉 The Complexity Index →

Authority 71 / 100

The vast one. Catalogs how much there is to hold in one's head at once. Deeply respected, rarely read for pleasure — its own length is the strongest entry it contains.
Three indexes. One index of indexes. We considered an index of indexes of indexes and elected, for the first time in project history, to stop. Not holy writ — pure outlet. Accurate under the humor.
