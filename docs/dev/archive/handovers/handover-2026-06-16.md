  on THAT source (gated via `enabled`/`per`), authored at its pass. **Nothing inverts onto the bonus.**

## ⛔ MODEL LAW — locked, carry forward (the most load-bearing for the next infos)

- **Containment spine** (`building-cascade-conversion.md:281`): `… resource → bonus → plot → feature →
  improvement → building → unit`. Bonus is above plot/feature/improvement/building.
- **Scopes** = `world | team | empire | area | city | plot{improvement|feature|terrain|route} | building |
  specialist | unit`. **`area` = continents/landmasses; `vicinity` = CITY scope** ("the city can WORK the
  resource", `hasVicinityBonus`); trade-connected bonus = team/empire (high on the spine). (enabler-spec §8 —
  CORRECTED this session; do not trust any older "area = vicinity" wording.)
- **CREST = keep-on-source** (modifier-spec §6): a bonus/religion/civic-conditioned effect STAYS on the source,
  referencing the conditioner via `enabled:{type:…, scope:…, min:1}` (presence) or `per:{type:…}` (count).
  **Nothing inverts onto the conditioner.** Sources/enablers (bonus/civic/religion/tech/trait) are NEVER targets.
- **Modifiers are TOP-DOWN only.** The `scope` on a deposit is the **modifier's** (where the effect applies); the
  condition is just "do we have X" (a tally count read), origin-agnostic.
- **The `enabled`/`requires` atom is FULL + EXPLICIT + self-describing** `{type, scope, min/max}` (enabler-spec
  §6.1) — never a bare `{min:1}`. The **tally** (engine machinery, NOT data) does the counting; data only
  declares THAT a count is needed and at what scope.
- The rest of the locked law (object-evaluated predicates, `SELF`, `requires.noneOf`, `per` `each`/`anyOf`,
  `grants` numeric pulses, shared `engine.property_source_v3`) is in handover-2026-06-15-night §"MODEL LAW" + the
  two specs — read them.
